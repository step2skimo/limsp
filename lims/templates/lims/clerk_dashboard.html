ISO/IEC 17025:2017 Compliance Framework

Quality Management Responsibilities
  • Maintain accurate and complete sample records
  • Ensure proper chain of custody documentation
  • Verify client information and sample integrity
  • Follow documented procedures for sample handling
Data Integrity & Traceability
  • All entries must be traceable and auditable
  • Sample codes must be unique and sequential
  • Maintain confidentiality of client information
  • Report any non-conformities immediately
System Notice: This Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is designed to support ISO/IEC 17025:2017 requirements for competence of testing and calibration laboratories. All operations are logged for quality assurance and regulatory compliance.
